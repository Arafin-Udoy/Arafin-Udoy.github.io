BIOGRAPHY

Greetings! As a recent graduate from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), I am eager to embark on a Ph.D. journey that will fulfill my dreams and advance my research goals to new levels. I possess a deep enthusiasm for research and have experience as a research assistant at the Applied Mechanics Lab, where I received guidance from Dr. Muhammad Ashiqur Rahman, a renowned figure leading the Mechanical Engineering Department at BUET. Currently, I am benefiting from the valuable mentorship of Dr. Sumon Saha at the Computational Fluid Dynamics & Heat Transfer Lab as I work towards publishing my inaugural research paper. In this role, I am refining my skills and immersing myself in the world of scientific exploration.

During my undergraduate studies, I chose a unique path, exploring advanced areas such as machine design, solid mechanics, and fracture mechanics with great enthusiasm. My goal was to create an affordable and user-friendly Horizontal Tensile Testing Machine. However, my hands-on experiences extended beyond this project. I actively engaged in various activities that involved unraveling the intricacies of control engineering, heat transfer, fluid dynamics, and engineering mathematics.

My interests span a wide range of subjects including fluid mechanics, aerodynamics, machine design, solid mechanics, hypersonic flow, control engineering, propulsion, turbo machinery, and thermodynamics. This diverse set of fields captivates me, inspiring me to dive deeper into the pool of knowledge where innovation and exploration await. As I embark on my Ph.D. journey, I aspire to reach for the highest achievements, driven by my dreams and a thirst for wisdom. With each step, I will carry the legacy of BUET's Mechanical Engineering Department and the guidance of my mentor, ready to confront any challenges and elevate my academic pursuits to new heights.
